Influences of France

Architecure, seasonings/dressings, and art or literature. Stone/brick, high roofs, and gothic styles are all french building styles. Did you know that mustard orgiginated from France(excuse the spelling)? Mustard came from Dijon France and is now used for hotdogs, sandwiches, and other foods. Some famous french artist were Claude Monet, Edgar Degas, Paul Cezanne, and Henri Matisse. Some of their works include Water Lily, The basket of apples and many more.
